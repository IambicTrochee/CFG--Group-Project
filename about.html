ABOUT RYAN

Ryan O'Riordan is a singer and songwriter from London. Pulling influence from Jazz and Soul, his music lets your feet free and your ears screaming for more.

Currently recording an EP with Jack Hudson for function work and more solo shows. Ryan sings with The Happy Hour Collective (a collection of bands that put their own swing on the pop genre), where he performs as a lead singer as well as arranging and performing in their Barbershop Quintet. Outside of the band, Ryan is breaking his way into London's open mic venues and artist clubs and has recently performed in the Phoenix Theatre's Artists Club.
