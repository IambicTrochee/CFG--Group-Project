ISLINGTON CHOIR

Some of the workshops went so well that the students didn't want to leave! And so they came up with a solution: to put together their own choir, led by Ryan, where they can continue learning and progressing, while singing songs that they've picked and want to sing. We meet up every Sunday at the Islington Chinese Association, 5.30pm-7.30pm. Tea, coffee and biscuits provided to sweeten the deal!

It costs £5 to turn up for a one off session (if you can't commit to every week) OR a months membership only costs £12!

If you're interested or would like to know more, pop by on Sunday! We're at:

Islington Chinese Association
21 Hatchard Road
London
N19 4NG
